About Me

I'm Justin Soderstrom, a Software Engineer currently living in Pierre, South Dakota. I got started into software development when I was 15, making small programs for fun. However, I didn't really get serious about development until I started college at Daktoa State University, where I graduated with a 4.0 in Computer Science.

I use this website to not only document what I learn on my technical escapades, but to share it. My goal is to write short, clear, and easy-to-follow guides for beginner/intermediate software engineers that make no assumptions about skill level.

I strongly believe in the value of education and the free sharing of information, which is why you will never see ads, popups, or sponsorships on my site.

Outside of the technical realm, I love to play video games, hike, and occassionally travel.
